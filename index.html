As per latest RBI guidelines, your banks may disable your credit / debit cards for online transactions by default if –

  1. (a) your card is newly issued / renewed / re - issued, or
  2. (b) your card has not been used for online transactions.

SBI BANK – CREDIT CARD

  1. (a) Login to SBI Card app / website
  2. (b) Navigate to “Cards Usage” section
  3. (c) Click on “Manage card transactions”
  4. (d) Enable your card
  5. (e) Enter OTP to confirm changes

SBI BANK – DEBIT CARD

  1. (a) Login to SBI YONO app / internet banking portal
  2. (b) Navigate to “Service Requests” section
  3. (c) Click on “ATM / Debit cards > Usage”
  4. (d) Enable your card
  5. (e) Enter OTP to confirm changes

ICICI BANK – CREDIT CARD

  1. (a) Log in to the ICICI mobile app / internet banking
  2. (b) Navigate to ‘Cards’ section
  3. (c) Click on ‘Manage Card`
  4. (d) Enable your card

Note: Internet banking users may have to verify the request with an OTP.

ICICI BANK – DEBIT CARD

  1. (a) Log in to the ICICI mobile app / internet banking
  2. (b) Navigate to “Card Services”
  3. (c) Select “Modify Debit Card Limit” option
  4. (d) Select "Domestic card limit" option
  5. (e) Set the limit and enable your card

HDFC BANK – CREDIT CARD

  1. (a) Login to HDFC internet banking
  2. (b) Under “Cards” option select “Request > Set Card Usage”
  3. (c) Enable your card.

Note: Internet banking users may have to verify the request with an OTP

HDFC BANK – DEBIT CARD

  1. (a) Login to HDFC internet banking
  2. (b) Navigate to Debit Cards section and select “Request > Set Card usage”
  3. (c) Under “Daily Domestic usage > Online Usage, select “ON” button to enable the card

Note: Internet banking users may have to verify the request with an OTP

AXIS BANK – CREDIT CARD

  1. (a) Login to your Axis mobile app/internet banking
  2. (b) Under ‘Services’, click on “Manage credit card usage > Domestic Usage”
  3. (c) Enable your card and submit

AXIS BANK – DEBIT CARD

  1. (a) Login to your Axis mobile app
  2. (b) Under ‘Services’ option, select “Manage usage”
  3. (c) Select the debit card and click “Continue”
  4. (c) Enable your card

KOTAK BANK – DEBIT CARD

    To Activate service via mobile banking app

  1. (a) Login to kotak mobile banking app
  2. (b) Go to service request
  3. (c) Tap on debit card
  4. (d) Go to card control
  5. (e) Select debit card cumber
  6. (f) Select international/domestic controls
  7. (g) Enable <Ecom/ Online / international usage/ contactless/ tokenization*> & confirm

    8. To Activate service via net nanking

  8. (a) Login to kotak net banking
  9. (b) Visit cards section
  10. (c) Click on card control
  11. (d) Select debit card number
  12. (e) Enable <Ecom/ Online / international usage/ contactless/ tokenization*> & confirm

KOTAK BANK – CREDIT CARD

  1. (a) Login to the ‘Kotak Mobile Banking App’ or ‘Net Banking’
  2. (b) Tap on ‘Credit Card’
  3. (c) Card ‘Overview
  4. (d) Enter ‘MPIN’
  5. (e) Click on ‘Manage Card’
  6. (f) Card Controls
  7. (g) Unlock Card
  8. (h) Enable ‘Domestic & International transactions.
  9. (i) Set Domestic & International limits.
